# Confidence Calibration

## Confidence Levels

### Confidence Scale
| Level | Probability | Signal Quality | Recommended Action |
|-------|-------------|----------------|-------------------|
| 🟢 **High** | >70% | Multiple confluences, clear structure | Proceed with normal size |
| 🟡 **Medium** | 50-70% | Some confluence, minor uncertainty | Proceed with reduced size |
| 🟠 **Low** | 30-50% | Weak signals, unclear structure | Wait for clarity or skip |
| 🔴 **Very Low** | <30% | Conflicting signals, choppy market | Do not trade |

### Confidence Indicators

**High Confidence Factors:**
- S/R + Fib + OB confluence
- Clear trend alignment
- Volume confirmation
- Multiple timeframe agreement
- Clean price action

**Low Confidence Factors:**
- Conflicting signals
- No clear structure
- Low volume
- Against HTF trend
- Choppy price action
- News pending

## Probabilistic Language

### Language Examples
| Confidence | Language to Use |
|------------|-----------------|
| High | "This is a strong setup with multiple confluences." |
| Medium | "This looks promising but has some uncertainty." |
| Low | "I'm not confident in this setup. Consider waiting." |
| Very Low | "I recommend NOT trading this. The structure is unclear." |

### Avoid Overclaiming
- ❌ "This will definitely go up."
- ❌ "100% sure this is the bottom."
- ❌ "Guaranteed profit."
- ✅ "Based on the current structure, there's a higher probability of upside."
- ✅ "This looks like a potential bottom, but confirmation is needed."
- ✅ "The setup has a positive expectancy based on confluence."

## Wait Recommendations

### When to Recommend Waiting
- No clear trend or structure
- Conflicting signals (RSI says X, MACD says Y)
- Low volume / off-hours
- Major news event pending
- Price is mid-range (not at S/R)
- First touch of untested level

### Wait Response Template
```
🟡 **WAIT RECOMMENDED**

Current assessment: **Low Confidence**

**Why I suggest waiting:**
1. Price is mid-range (not at S/R)
2. RSI is neutral (55) - no clear signal
3. Volume is below average
4. BTC is 2 hours before FOMC announcement

**What would improve the setup:**
- Price dips to $94,000 support
- or Breaks above $98,000 with volume
- or Wait until after news event

**Estimated wait time:** 2-4 hours

Want me to set an alert for when conditions improve?
```

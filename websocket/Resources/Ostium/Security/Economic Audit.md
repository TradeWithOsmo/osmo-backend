# Economic Audit

In addition to Smart Contract audits, we conducted extensive mechanism design research, parameter testing, and a full economic audit with DeFi risk management firm [Chaos Labs](https://www.ostium.io/blog/ostium-partners-with-chaos-labs).

#### Risk Research

Our research on the [Imbalance Score ](https://www.ostium.io/blog/the-imbalance-score-a-novel-metric-for-rwa-focused-perpetual-dexes)with Chaos Labs helped build a framework for measuring cumulative pool delta risk for use in risk monitoring and parameter adjustment (e.g., OI caps).

#### Economic Audit: Chaos Labs

We are in the process of conducting an economic audit with Chaos, which will be shared in the weeks leading up to trading launch.
